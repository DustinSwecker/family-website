Becoming a Parent

Choosing to become a parent is a huge life-altering decision. You quickly go from complete freedom and carefree living to running around all over the place at the whims of a dictatorial toddler! There are numerous things to think of before you endeavor to make the decision:


  • What sort of support system do you have?
  • Do you have the financial wherewithal to handle the incoming responsibility?
  • Is your significant other up to the challenge?
  • Do you have the space for more people in your house?
  • Are you prepared to make the substantial time commitment to raising wee ones?

Obviously, the list could go on, but these are the sort things we need to think about in order to prepare. A good way to prepare yourself is to develop your own list.

Take the time to jot down some of your own questions!
